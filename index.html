A little about me

I'm a self-taught web developer from Nigeria. My journey started in 2016 when I built my first website using Wix. Then I stumbled upon WordPress, and that’s when things really took off for me in web design and development. I wanted more control over what I was creating, so I got into frontend technologies. But being curious, I couldn’t resist checking out how the backend works, which led me to learn Node.js. I love designing and building cool digital experiences that not only look good but also work smoothly and keep people engaged.

...oh ! did i forget to mention that I also love playing with graphic, UI/UX design and digital tools like GIMP, Inkscape, Canva, Figma, Sketch, Blender and Kapwing

Here, I have gathered some of my coolest projects. Each one's a mix of creativity, skill and pure passion.
